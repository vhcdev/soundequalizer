FAQ

What is MVEO ?
MVEO is an online service, which analyses and adjusts the volume of your mp3 files, so that they have the same volume.
Which audio formats are supported by Music Volume Equalizer Online ? (MVEO)
Currently we support only .mp3 format. The next release will support a wide range of formats like (ogg, wav, mp4, aac...).
How many files can I upload ?
Withou registration you are limited to upload 5 files. After registration you can upload as many files as you want.
What benefit do i have when i register ?
You can upload as many files as you want. Your music files will not be deleted after closing your browser. You can share your files to multiple devices.
Can i close my browser during uploading ?
No. It is important that you have your browser open during uploading process.
I want to control the volume. How can i do it?
We are building the control volume button and it will be available soon.
