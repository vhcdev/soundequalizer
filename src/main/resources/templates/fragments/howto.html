How to

  • Upload files

    Your files will be uploaded to our cloud storage. Because of server cost we are currently limiting to upload up to maximal 5 mp3 files for free use.

  • Choose volume

    Your ears are happy with 89 db and it is our standard value.

  • Run the equalizer

    The equalizer analyses and adjusts the volume of your audio files. After that you will never have to touch your volume button a gain.

  • Download

    After equalizing you can download individual files or all files as zip.
